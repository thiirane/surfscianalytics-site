Who We Are

Bridging Science and Engineering for Your Success
Hello, Im Dr. Russell Crook, President and founder of Surface Science Analytics. Ive spent over 30 years working in materials analytics, with a special focus on polymeric, composite, and adhesive materials. As the founder of Surface Science Analytics, Ive had the opportunity to help clients solve complex technical challenges by combining chemistry and engineering mechanics. Over the years, this includes working with both small and large pharmaceutical device manufacturers, focusing on surface physics, computer vision, and predictive analytics. My experience also includes work in counter-space intelligence and OPIR research at the National Geospatial Intelligence Agency, and more recently, Ive partnered with the U.S. Air Force to develop advanced methods for optimizing Ballistic Missile Defense. Im passionate about using innovative solutions to make a real impact across a variety of industries. If you're facing a challenging project or looking for expert guidance, Id love to connect and explore how we can work together to achieve your business goals.
