Micro-Mechanics: Particle-Matrix Interactions & Damage Evolution
A finite element model based on microscopic analysis of a uniaxial test specimen of filled hyper-elastic material, showing high hydrostatic stress between aluminum particles in the matrix during extension, highlighting the tendency of highly filled polymers to develop distributed damage.
3 Pump Laser Combiner Micro-optic Device
Durable UV curable adhesively bonded joints for micro-optic packaging were developed, along with an environmentally friendly surface treatment that withstood 2000 hours of 85C and 85% RH aging, exhibiting less than 0.05dB insertion loss.
Machine Learning: Vehicle Classifier Training
developed support vector machines (SVM) that used supervised learning to rapidly identify types of vehicles in video streams. A technique known as Histogram Oriented Gradient (HOG) was used to create a SVM that could identified viehicles based on the statistics of their shapes.
